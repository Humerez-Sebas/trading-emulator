# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Traders (from beginners to advanced) who need to practice their trading strategies through market replay (backtesting) using historical data, without risking real capital.

## Product Purpose
A trading emulator that provides a realistic but distraction-free environment to replay market data candle by candle, place orders (SL/TP), and track session metrics. Success means the user feels as if they are trading live, but with a highly focused, modern tool.

## Brand Personality
Modern, dark, and technical. The voice is professional and objective. Emotional goals: instill focus, confidence, and clarity, avoiding the anxiety of cluttered interfaces.

## Anti-references
- Do not look like MetaTrader 4 (outdated, cluttered, overly complex).
- Avoid unnecessary noise, sidebars, or menus that distract from the main chart and metrics.

## Design Principles
1. **Focus on the Data:** The chart and metrics are the hero. Everything else should recede.
2. **Technical Elegance:** Use a native dark mode that feels premium, technical, and precise (similar to TradingView, but cleaner).
3. **Clarity over Density:** Avoid cramming information. Give UI elements room to breathe.
4. **Actionable Metrics:** Data should be perfectly legible with high contrast, enabling quick decision making.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard accessibility (WCAG AA). Absolute priority on perfect contrast for charts, metrics, and text against dark backgrounds to ensure readability during long sessions.
